Simple, universal meeting planner

Who is this for?

This is for people who want a simple, hassle free way to schedule meetings with friends. When Are You Free works across timezones and allows basic collaboration to find the best meeting time possible.

How do I use it?

To use it, tap the button to create a new meeting. Give the meeting a title and date. Then hit submit.

You will be given a link to share with your meeting partners. They will be able to suggest a time or vote on a previously suggested time. Times will automatically be converted between timezones.
